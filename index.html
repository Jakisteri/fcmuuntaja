Unit Conversion Toolbox

Welcome to Unit Conversion Toolbox!


This site is still in progress, but it's growing slow and steady one unit conversion tool at a time. The future goal of Unit Converion Toolbox is to serve as a quick way to convert many of the most commonly used units. Focus of possible future development will include adding conversion or other tools that I personally need most in my life. Developing own conversion tools in form of a web page helps you to better understand website development in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, but you can also learn more about physical quantities and formulas that underlie them.
